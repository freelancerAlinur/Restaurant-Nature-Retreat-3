Our Story

Hotel Rivel is more than a luxury retreat—it's a Costa Rican non-profit organization dedicated to sustainable tourism and community empowerment.

Run entirely by local Costa Ricans, we embody the true spirit of Pura Vida while providing world-class hospitality. Our commitment to environmental conservation and social responsibility ensures that every stay contributes to the preservation of this natural paradise.
